Kim Kardashian Claps Back After Taylor Swift Calls Her A Bully!

 Posted on February 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  531 words  |  Martina Birk

Just when we thought the Kim Kardashian / Taylor Swift feud era was put to rest, not a chance.

After Taylor subtly dissed the reality star and her husband, Kanye West, in her cover interview with Elle Magazine, we all buckled up and got ready for the ride we are all so tired of being on. 

But at the same time, we’re so here for the bumps, hills and everything in between.

Taylor reignited the past feud after she took the time to share with Elle 30 lessons she has learned over the years, ahead of her 30th birthday which isn’t until December 13. 

The Reputation singer had a lot to say about the 38-year-old mother. A lot of things Kim did not like. 

“According to my birth certificate, I turn 30 this year. It’s weird because part of me still feels 18 and part of me feels 283, but the actual age I currently am is 29,” Taylor began in her tell-all interview.

“But until then, I thought that I’d share some lessons I’ve learned before reaching 30 because its 2019 and sharing is caring,” Tay added.

And before we knew it, shade was being thrown and drama was gettin’ stirred. 

Without naming names, Taylor still called out the Kardashian while explaining the importance of having a backbone and not being taken advantage of, writing, “Trust your gut and know when to strike back. Be like a snake-only bite if someone steps on you.”

In case you forgot, back in 2016 Kim called Taylor a snake on a viral Snapchat video, which urged fans of the Keeping Up with the Kardashians star to comment snakes on her social, prompting Taylor to create her snake-themed tour for Reputation.  

Kim called her a snake after Taylor played ‘the victim’ after husband Kanye wrote his song “Famous” which featured the lyrics: “I feel like me and Taylor might still have sex, Why? I made that bitch famous.”

Taylor claimed she never approved of him using those lyrics, though Kim posted ‘proof’ that she most definitely did.

It’s really some petty drama, especially for a mother of three and a successful musician who is about to turn 30-years-old.

Put that on top of the Kanye and Taylor Grammy incident, we totally understand her beef with the Kardashian/West duo. 

Taylor also told Elle she learned that “disarming someone’s petty bullying can be as simple as learning to laugh,” adding that “A few years ago, someone started an online hate campaign by calling me a snake on the internet.”

After Kim got word of Taylor’s insulting interview, she was fuming and ready to slap back. 

“Taylor can talk all the s–t she wants because Kim is done with her and her petty games,” a source close to the TV personality told Radar.

“Taylor only brings Kim and Kanye’s name up when no one is paying attention to her and it is just sad,” the insider added.

“But Kim is not going to say anything and she is definitely not going to apologize to Taylor.

“She just really thinks that Taylor needs to grow the f–k up and stop bringing her back into her narrative.”

Ouch. This is a lot, even for us.
