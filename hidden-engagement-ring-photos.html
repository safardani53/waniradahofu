Edi Okoro knew he had to do something unexpected to propose to his girlfriend Cally...so he took the ring with him everywhere and posted hilarious pics of it just out of her sight.

How does one even decide to propose anymore? It's all been done! The grand public gestures, the flash mobs, the ring hidden in the cake, the intimate, candlelit dinner... It's hard to come up with a unique proposal these days. And more than being unique, it needs to speak to who you are as a couple. 

Edi Okoro was thinking about all of this when he was trying to figure out how to propose to his girlfriend, Cally. He carried around the ring for months waiting for the perfect moment. But he decided to have a little fun in the process, taking pics of the ring just out of Cally's view. Sometimes, he got so close I can't believe she didn't catch him. Edi posted these photos on Facebook, where they went totally viral because they are just about the cutest things you will ever see.

"You want to propose in a way that speaks to you as a person and couple," Edi wrote in the Facebook post that accompanies these hilarious pictures. "Some plan a flash mob, a fancy meal, or even arrange a marry me sign. I couldn't do this because 'Edi doesn't plan'... I'm a spontaneous improviser!"

He knew that if he planned something special, Cally would immediately catch on to what he was going to do. So he took the ring with him everywhere and waited for the right moment to pop the question.

"After a month or so of waiting for the right moment," he wrote, "I was sitting on the sofa admiring 'my precious' (yes I became very attached to this ring by this point). Cally almost caught me staring at the ring, and it hit me! ... Cally doesn't know I have the ring!! I should just start documenting these moments!"
