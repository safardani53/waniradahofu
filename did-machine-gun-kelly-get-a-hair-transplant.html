MACHINE Gun Kelly has made headlines in recent weeks over his relationship with actress Megan Fox.

Who is Machine Gun Kelly?

Machine Gun Kelly is an American rapper, singer and songwriter.

He is originally from Cleveland, Ohio.

Although he goes by Machine Gun Kelly, the rapper's real name is Colson Baker.

He's released five different studio albums, the first of which was put out in 2012.

The rapper has collaborated on songs with other famous names, including Kid Rock, Camila Cabello, Quavo, Ty Dolla $ign, Yungblud, and Hailee Steinfeld.

His most recent single, Bloody Valentine, was released earlier this year.

The music video for the song featured actress and girlfriend Megan Fox, 34.

Did he get a hair transplant?

Kelly's hair has appeared fuller and thicker in recent pictures.

His hair stylist shared that the rapper admitted to having thin hair, according to the blog Hair Dryer Fair.

To avoid his fear of going bald, MGK reportedly decided to get a hair transplant.

Before pictures show the rapper with a hairline that was farther back on his forehead, and far thinner.

Recent pictures, however, show a thick, full hairline.

What is a hair transplant?

In a hair transplant, a plastic dermatological surgeon moves hair – often from the back or side of the head – to the front, according to Healthline.

Hair is either moved as a strip of skin is cut from the back of the head, and closed with stitches, or hair follicles are cut out and placed in individual holes on the part of the scalp getting the transplant.

This process can take three to ten sessions, which must be done months apart, according to Healthline.

Procedures range from around $4,000 to $15,000 each session.
