Photos Of The Stars Festive Looks Hollywood Life

 Posted on March 16, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  666 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The holiday season is finally here! After the dismal year that’s been 2020, we could all use some cheer, and these stars are ready to deliver in healthy doses. Not only have fans been checking out the beautiful decorations that the stars have put together for the coming holidays, they’ve also seen their fresh, festive style. Whether it’s casual attire, a dressed-up look, or maybe some cozy pajamas, check out what celebs have been wearing for this holiday season!

Reese Witherspoon

Reese Witherspoon was seriously serving some feel-good holiday vibes when she and her look-a-like daughter, Ava Phillippe, wore mother-daughter matching holiday sweaters on December 7! The pair looked extra festive with a warm mug of their favorite drink in their hands and they even matched their makeup, with both sporting a gorgeous red lip. The Oscar-winner did, however, confess that she “had to beg [Ava] to wear a matching Holiday sweater….but isn’t it CUTE??!!” We totally agree, Reese!

Jennifer Lopez

Fans of Jennifer Lopez were completely transported to a winter wonderland when her beauty company, J Lo Beauty, shared this image on November 26. The “In The Morning” singer luxuriated on the couch while rocking a stunning white monochromatic ensemble. The oversized top featured an off-the-shoulder neckline with sparkling silver details. With her hair pulled up in a tight bun, J Lo looked like the epitome of holiday elegance.

Julianne Hough

There was absolutely no doubt about how excited Julianne Hough is for the holidays. The Dancing With The Stars alum took to Instagram to show off her cute and casual holiday style with some retro inspiration. Julianne wore a beautiful red blouse with a pair of fitted black pants as she sat on the floor and tossed some Christmas goodies in the air. With Brenda Lee‘s “Rockin’ Around The Christmas Tree” playing in the background, Julianne offered some ’60s style with her hair done in a vintage cut.

Shawn Johnson

Let’s face it, the holidays are about getting cozy with the ones you love. In one of her recent Instagram posts, former Olympian Shawn Johnson cozied-up to her little sugar plum, one-year-old daughter Drew Hazel East, in adorable Christmas pjs. Shawn looked great in her green top and white pants patterned with winter iconography. Little Hazle looked extra sweet in her little red number with a matching headband, too!

Kyle Richards

Much like Shawn, Kyle Richards was also in a snuggly frame of mind when she and her daughters wore matching red silk pajamas! The ladies looked so festive, as they got together for their annual holiday card. “It’s beginning to look a lot like Christmas,” an excerpt from Kyle’s caption read.

January Jones

Since acting on Mad Men, January Jones has definitely had a knack for rocking some amazing vintage style. While posing in front of her decorated Christmas tree on November 21, the actress showed off her flapper-inspired LBD! January twirled around in the fabulous frock, which featured plenty of tassels on the bottom of her dress and around the sleeves. This look would have been the envy of any holiday get-together!

Mariah Carey

Everyone knows that Mariah Carey is the reigning Queen of Christmas — and for good reason! The Grammy-winner’s holiday style is always on point, as are her beautiful holiday hits. The “All I Want For Christmas Is You” singer’s outfit on November 29 let her legions of fans know that she was ready “to kick the season into hiiiigh gear!” Mariah wore a glittering red dress and beautiful jewels on her wrist and fingers. Her holiday-inspired outfits are always the gifts that just keep on giving!

Carrie Underwood

Finally, Carrie Underwood gave her fans a glimpse of her own holiday fashion the same day that her HBO Max special, My Gift: A Christmas Special From Carrie Underwood, debuted on the streaming platform. The country crooner looked luminous in her Instagram Live video. Carrie wore a red blouse with gold polka dots and black pants. Her signature long blonde locks cascaded down her shoulders and the singer looked like a beautiful Christmas angel!
