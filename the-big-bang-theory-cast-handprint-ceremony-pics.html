‘The Big Bang Theory’ cast was honored with a handprint ceremony in Los Angeles back in 2019. See photos of the epic cast event.

The Big Bang Theory became one of the biggest television hits after it premiered in 2007. The show ran for 12 seasons and starred Jim Parsons, Mayim Bialik, Johnny Galecki, Kaley Cuoco, Kunal Nayyar, Melissa Rauch, and more.

The show came to an end in 2019. By that time, The Big Bang Theory prequel series Young Sheldon had already premiered. Iain Armitage took on the role of the younger Sheldon Cooper, with Jim reprising his role as the adult version and serving as the show’s narrator.
