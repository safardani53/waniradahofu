Quincy Lamont Williams, known as Peewee Longway, was an American hip hop artist, was one of the last members of 1017 Records before Gucci founder Mane was jailed in 2013-2016. He is also the grandson of Brick Squad member Cold Blooded Da Don, who introduced him to Gucci Mane. The artist's most famous releases to date are the mixtape The Blue M&M and his collaboration with Young Thug, "Loaded". Blue M&M also performed singles "Sneakin n Geekin" and "Servin Lean" with A $ AP Rocky. He is also a member of a rap group with Gucci Mane and Young Dolph, collectively known as "Felix Brothers".
