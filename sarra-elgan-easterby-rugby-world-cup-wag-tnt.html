Meet Rugby World Cup Wag and TNT Sports presenter Sarra Elgan Easterby as fans say you look as ho

 Posted on June 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  278 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

SARRA ELGAN EASTERBY sparked a hit wave on social media as fans labelled the Rugby World Cup presenter "as hot as the summer".

Fans raved as the wife of Ireland defence coach Simon Easterby shared a "leggy" and "gorgeous" mini-skirt pic in front of the Eiffel Tower in Paris.

Rugby is almost as much a pillar of her family as the tower is to the French capital.

Dad Elgan Rees played for Wales and husband Simon is an Irish legend - both also featuring for the British Lions.

Now Sarra is a key presenter for TNT Sports, having also worked for S4C and interviewed celebrities like Britney Spears.

And she warmed up for Friday night's tournament opener between France and New Zealand by sparking a sizzling response from her 50,300 Instagram followers.

The mum-of-two by posted a sunny snap and "Bonjour" greetings from the 35 degrees' heat of Paris.

The first-class graduate in Theatre, Music and Media will be in the spotlight perhaps more than ever - as hosts France and her hubbie's Ireland are well backed to challenge for the crown.

Posing in high-heels, a black skirt and matching waistcoat, Sarra was called "Magnifique" and "La Perfection".

She might appreciate those efforts at bilingual praise as she is a fluent Welsh speaker.

Many fans singled out her cool-looking image.

"Adore this waistcoat", said one, and "Love this outfit," commented another.

And as rugby enthusiasts are hoping Ireland keep up their scorching form on the pitch, Sarra's fans are delighted with her efforts to show off the Indian Summer sweeping across Europe.

This view spoke for many: "Hot! You and the weather x."
