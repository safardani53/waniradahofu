Bio:
Chelsea Houska (born Chelsea Anne Houska on August 29, 1991 in Vermillion, South Dakota) gained attention as one of the main cast members on the MTV series Teen Mom 2. She previously appeared on 16 & Pregnant. Chelsea’s dream was to become a hairstylist, and after dropping out of high school to give birth to her daughter, she was able to graduate cosmetology school on Nov. 15, 2013. Chelsea obtained her GED in 2013.

Best Known For:
Chelsea Houska is best known for being on 16 & Pregnant and Teen Mom 2.

Personal Life:
Chelsea has one child, Aubree, with ex-boyfriend, Adam Lind. She gave birth to Aubree on Sept. 7, 2009. Chelsea and Adam struggled over custody of their daughter and reached an emotional point when Adam started a family with his new girlfriend. She married Cole DeBoer in 2016 and gave birth to their first child, Watson Cole, a few months later. In March 2018, she announced that she was pregnant with her third child.
