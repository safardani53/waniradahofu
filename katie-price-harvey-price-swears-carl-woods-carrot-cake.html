Katie Price's son Harvey swears at Carl Woods as he pinches his carrot cake in home video

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  274 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

KATIE Price's son Harvey swore at Carl Woods after he pinched his carrot cake in a funny home video.

The 18-year-old could be seen sitting at a desk with a box full of colouring pencils in front of him while a train video played on a screen beside it.

Carl filmed himself picking up a Tesco Finest carrot cake and going "mmmm" as if he was going to eat it without Harvey.

But the teen was not messing about when it came to his sweet treat as he told the 31-year-old: "No Carl it's f***ing Harvey's carrot cake."

Carl asked if he wanted the cake back and Harvey replied: "Yes please…for f***s sake."

Chuckling to himself, Carl handed the box over and Harvey replied: "Thank you."

Katie, 42, captioned the video on her Instagram: "Harvey at his happiest with a box of colouring pens his iPad his computer and carrot cake" alongside some crying with laughter emojis.

Her followers were equally amused by Harvey's passion for his favourite cake, with one writing: "Harvey you are a legend!"

Another added: "I'd be the same with carrot cake."

A third commented: "Oh Harvey you just say it how it is, good for you."

The video comes days after Katie shared a video of her enjoying the country life with Carl.

In the Instagram story her dog Precious wrestled with Carl's rescue dog Sid, while Katie's other dog, Blade, ran around the yard.

"Country life with family dogs and building a future is what I love best and doing it with Carl Woods completes everything," Katie wrote alongside a love-heart eyed emoji.
