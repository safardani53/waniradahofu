Aldi has the perfect $3.99 dupe for a $15 bottle of wine - it tastes almost exactly the same

 Posted on September 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  446 words  |  Larita Shotwell

AN ALDI superfan has revealed the perfect $3.99 dupe for a $15 bottle of wine that she said tastes almost exactly the same.

The TikToker pointed out that Stella Rosa Blueberry Wine retails for about $15 while Aldi's version goes for only $4.

Lauryn Chayce Coleman, who goes by @laurenchayce on the app, shared the dupe in a recent video.

She is seen pouring a glass of the Stella Rosa Blueberry Wine, which she described as "sweet."

Then, she poured a glass of the Aldi wine, Pacific Fruit Vineyards Sweet Blueberry.

"This wine tastes almost EXACTLY like Stella Rosa, for only $3.99 per bottle!" Coleman wrote.

Fans said they were eager to try the dupe in the comments.

"I'm putting that s**t in the blender," one person said.

"I wish I had an Aldi's near me," said another.

"I’m going to Aldi’s," a third wrote.

According to Stella Rosa's website, the blueberry wine "is undeniably irresistible."

"Its sweet and succulent blueberry taste will elevate your mood and your wine-tasting expectations," the winemaker wrote.

The description added that it "pairs well with fresh fruits, BBQ chicken, charcuterie, cheesecake, and cinnamon roll coffee cake."

It retails for $12.95 directly from the winery, and $9.98 and $10.99 from Walmart and Target respectively.

Aldi wrote that Pacific Fruits Wine allows customers to "experience a fruity, medium-bodied wine with delicious sweet natural flavors of vibrant blueberry. "

The grocer said the wine is a "perfect complement to a delicious meal, beautiful day, fun party, or on its own."

It should be served chilled.
