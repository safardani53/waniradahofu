Paris, who’s juggling her show with her various other business ventures (she had a photo shoot for her shoe collection in L.A. on Monday, for example), says she’s loving her fab physique. “I’m really happy,” she says. “I’ve been working out and eating really healthy. I’m not drinking any soda; I’m drinking lots of water.  No fast food, but a lot of organic food.”

Paris’ biggest void in her diet? “No fried food,” she says. “I cut out McDonald’s french fries! But I’ve lost over ten pounds in about two to three months.”

And her weight loss has been a family effort: “I have a trainer and we do weight training and cardio but I’ll also walk up Doheny [Drive] with my sister [Nicky], and I’ll go horseback riding.”

So will Paris spend the summer showing off her new body? Well, only if it meshes with her busy work schedule, which includes filming her new show: “I’ll be working a lot this summer. Like I’ll work on finishing my album. I’ll also go to Malibu, Europe, London, Ibiza and Italy.”

And Paris has no qualms showing viewers her struggle to lose those few extra pounds: “I love doing reality,” Paris says. “I’ve been doing it for so long, and I really love to show people a peek into my world and see how I really am around my family and friends, to really have my fans get to know me.”
