TEEN Mom Kailyn Lowry has been spotted with what appears to be a baby bump in leaked photos from a birthday party.

Fans have feverishly speculated that the Teen Mom 2 alum may be pregnant with her fifth child.

Kailyn, 30, is seen in the background of the party, holding her youngest son Creed, two.

The photos also show her other son Lux, five, standing in front of her as she gently taps him on the shoulder encouraging him to participate in the party festivities.

Kail is seen wearing a blue sweatshirt, that hugs around her belly area.

Fans have been speculating for months that Kailyn is pregnant with her fifth child, and have spotted clues that she may be due soon.

COVERED STOMACH

In addition to apparent strategic photo placement, fans have noticed Kail has been wearing baggier clothes.

The U.S. Sun has obtained exclusive photos of Kailyn running errands at Target and Hobby Lobby with her boyfriend. 

In the photos, Kailyn wore a baggy graphic t-shirt with black leggings, as she covered her stomach with the oversized top. 

Her live-in boyfriend Elijah, 24, also dressed down for the errand run, as he wore a black and gray jacket, gray sweatpants and a black hat. 

The U.S. Sun obtained exclusive unedited photos in September of the former Teen Mom 2 star with an apparent bump leaving a Wawa convenience store in Delaware. 

Kailyn wore a t-shirt and black pants at the time. 

Fans were quick to comment on the photos, as one said on Reddit: “She’s definitely pregnant again.”
