YOU don’t have to shell out all your money for Easter this year – Dollar Tree has plenty of great Easter basket finds for just $1.25 available to shoppers.

A TikToker recently told her 300,000 followers about the crazy good deals you can score at the Dollar Tree.

Dollar Tree seasonally updates its product lineup – that means there are plenty of great Easter and spring items for just a little over a dollar this year.

Liz (@Liz_The_ClearanceQueen) regularly chronicles her couponing tales as a savvy shopper at stores like Dollar Tree and Walmart.

However, she was truly impressed by the offerings at Dollar Tree, especially if you’re looking to make a homemade Easter egg basket.

EASTER DISCOVERIES

First up, Liz found some adorable white and brown stuffed bunny toys, which would make the perfect edition for any child’s Easter basket.

Some other stuffed animals available included Berenstain bears for just $1.25.

If you want a more colorful bunny though, there’s a bright sea green one that is “perfect for the Easter basket,” Liz said.

Of course, no Easter basket is complete without some sweet treats scattered throughout.

That’s why Liz was drawn to the Double Bubble egg shaped bubble gum.

“These are going home with me,” Liz said. “They’re my fave.”

There were also some more unusual finds at the store, including a lollipop with an opening unicorn container and a bunny-shaped gumball machine.

For something the whole family can get behind, consider the Easter cookie decorating kit Dollar Tree has available.

It’s entirely ready-to-use with the frosting and sprinkles required to create an adorable Easter bunny cookie design.

Easter products available also include the Easter themed blow-pop minis.

And of course, you’ll need an actual basket to house all of these items.

Luckily, Dollar Tree has shoppers covered with a felt Easter basket in the style of a white bunny face with pink or blue ears.

But that’s just one of many available.

“Look at all of these baskets,” Liz said as she browsed the multicolored felt and plastic options available.

If your family celebrates Easter with the traditional Easter egg scavenger hunt, you’d also benefit from a trip to Dollar Tree.

There, you’ll uncover plastic eggs of all shades and sizes.

“What’s cheaper this year?” Liz asked her followers. “Plastic eggs or real eggs?”

Also available at the discount retailer today are Arthur Berenstain and Elinor Wonders Why water bottles, which kids young and old are sure to love.

And you might have thought you’ve seen it all at Dollar Tree, but you might be surprised to find a new way to celebrate Easter with the store’s Easter tree.

There’s also giant eggs for low prices.

“These are super cool,” Liz said.

The savvy shopper also discovered pop its and various Easter signs to get your house looking festive.

Outside of the Easter section, Dollar Tree had blender sponges in stock, mini fans and sand baskets.

Liz said these sand baskets can double up as both an Easter and beach trip essential.

“I like to do our Easter baskets in sand buckets,” Liz said.

You could also put the Sticker Activity Surprise toy or butterfly clips in your child’s Easter basket, depending on their preferences.

Other possible toy options include a stuffed Arthur character, mini etch a sketches, and Crafter’s squares.

Also available for $1.25 are wreath rails, craft supplies, planting pots and hanging wire baskets.

“The craft selection at this one is really nice,” Liz shared.

Dollar Tree truly has all a shopper could imagine when walking inside the store – Liz found herself impressed by the lightbulb terrariums the retailer has on display as well.

Meanwhile, children will likely be excited by Dollar Tree’s activity art sets for Disney princesses.

Or perhaps, a kite is more up their speed – several are available with all different types of character design.

To really create the perfect Easter egg basket, you’ll likely also need to pick up some Peeps.

Dollar Tree had the treat available in various flavors, including cotton candy.

And before you leave, be sure to check out Dollar Tree’s supply of Energizer batteries and air wicks.

SAVINGS AT DOLLAR TREE

Dollar Tree offers the vast majority of its products for just $1.25, easily making it one of the cheapest stores around for grocery or self-care products.

Cleaning supplies, baking goods, and toiletries are typically sold at significantly lower rates.

You can also find toys, party supplies, and health products to be some of the best deals.

Still, many other outlets offer surprising prices and sales, including Aldi and Walmart, especially if they’re unmarked clearance items.

There’s extra ways to boost your savings the next time you’re in any store though.

Look into cashback apps that offer rewards, as they can help you earn extra on groceries, toys and more.

Ibotta is one of the most popular ones to use, with the company claiming its users save an average of $150 per year.
