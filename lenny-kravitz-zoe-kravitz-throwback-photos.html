Zo Kravitz &amp; Lenny Kravitz Throwback Photos

 Posted on June 21, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  515 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Happy Birthday, Zoë Kravitz!

The eternally cool daughter of Lenny Kravitz and Lisa Bonet turns 35 on Dec. 1, 2023, and through the years, has shared many special moments with her mom and dad.

Lenny, in particular, has posted many a throwback with his daughter through the years, clearly proud of all she's accomplished. Here, take a look at some of the pair's most touching throwbacks, in honor of Zoë's latest milestone.

Oh, Baby!

When you're the daughter of Lisa Bonet and Lenny Kravitz, you're already exponentially cooler than everyone — celebrity or not.

Here are Zoë and her parents back in 1989, looking adorable and unbothered at a press conference in N.Y.C.

'Love of My Life'

On his daughter's 32nd birthday in 2020, the rocker shared a snuggly shot, captioning it, "Happy Birthday to the love of my life."

And for her 33rd, he posted this pic, with quite possibly the sweetest dad to daughter caption ever: "Happy Birthday, Zoë. So many more castles to build together. I love you endlessly."

Twice as Nice

The tiny cutie smiled extra wide as she was held by her daddy.

Red Carpet Royalty

The pair arrived at the 1997 MTV Video Music Awards in N.Y.C. matching in leather outfits. Dad topped his off with a bright purple hat, while Zoë wore strappy sandals.

Kiss for His Miss

Lenny planted a big kiss on his daughter's cheek at the Grammys in 2000. Also worth noting: Zoë's fashion-forward outfit, which is giving us so much life.

Dance Partners

"Another year of dancing through this life with you my love. The best is yet to come," the rocker captioned his sweet 31st birthday tribute to Zoë.

Get Glowing

Pre-teen Zoë Kravitz had us screaming. Her one-shoulder striped top and orange purse — worn to a screening party for 2002's The Groovenians at Lucky Cheng's — were too cute!

Smile Wide

Lenny and his little girl couldn't hold back their giggles at the Christian Dior Fashion Show during 2007's Paris Fashion Week.

Cool Club

The ever-stylish pair can out-dress any celebrity father-daughter duo, and this photo is proof.

Here's Looking at You

Love seemingly beamed from Zoë's eyes, straight into those of her father.

City Slickers

Dad Lenny held his little girl's hand at N.Y.C's Wild: Fashion Untamed exhibition gala opening in 2004.

Rock On

More kisses to spare at the 25th anniversary Rock & Roll Hall of Fame concert at Madison Square Garden in 2009.

Family Photo

This photo showcased how Zoë is the perfect blend of her stunning parents. Their looks are undeniable, but their strong bond as a family is even more evident.
