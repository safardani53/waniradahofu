Early Life, Family & Education

Kostas Antetokounmpo was born on November 20, 1997, to Nigerian parents, lt. Charles Antetokounmpo and Veronica Antetokounmpo.

His father was of Yoruba ethnicity, and his mother is from the Igbo Nigerian ethnic group.

Antetokounmpo comes from an athletic family. His father, Charles, was a national-level soccer player in Nigeria, and his mother, Veronica, was a high jumper.

His father and mother were immigrants in the Greek, where they lived in hardship, as employment was hardly available for the immigrants.

It eventually affected the family’s financial condition, making it harder to bolster the family with their income.

Because of such harsh situations, Antetokounmpo, with his brothers, sold watches, handbags, and sunglasses in the streets to take care of the family’s needs.

In 2016, Antetokounmpo became a full Greek citizen. He got a Nigerian passport in June 2013 to gain a visa to enter the US legitimately.

Tragically, he lost his father, Charles, in 2017 due to a heart attack.

NBA Career

Kostas Antetokounmpo was picked in the 2018 NBA Draft by the Philadelphia 76ers. However, he was traded off to the Dallas Mavericks quickly after that. 

The athlete signed a two-way contract on July 13; he divided his playing time between the Mavericks and Texas Legends of the NBA G League.

He debuted in NBA on March 20, 2019, against the Portland Trail Blazers, but his team lost the match. On July 19, 2019, Antetokounmpo was released by the Mavericks.

Kostas again signed a two-way contract with Los Angeles Lakers on July 22, 2019.

He played five games throughout the 2019-2020 season for the Lakers. They won the NBA championship after defeating Miami Heat and became the first Greek-born player to win an NBA championship.

The basketball player again signed a two-way contract with the Lakers on November 26, 2020.

On October 14, 2022, Chicago Bulls signed him in a two-way contract; however, he was waived by the Bulls on December 16, 2022.

He did not even play a single game for the team. Kostas signed with the team Fenerbahce of the Turkish Basketball Super League on December 19, 2022.
