Mike Kanellis Married to Maria Kanellis, Are They Happily Together? Know About Maria's Pregnancy

 Posted on June 19, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  488 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Mike Bennett, best known by his stage name Mike Kanellis, is currently married to his wife Mary Louis Kanellis, a.k.a. Maria Kanellis. Both of them are professional wrestlers and are signed to WWE. It has been 3 years since they got married and as of 2018, they are all set to welcome their first child into the world. They first announced that they were expecting their first child on 25th September 2017. 

Mike is currently 32-years-old whereas his wife is 35. Mike has an estimated net worth of $3 million, and Maria's estimated net worth is also $3 million. Combined they have an estimated net worth of $6 million which indeed makes them one of the WWE's power couples. 

Today, we are going to give insiders about Mike and Maria's marital life, their soon to be born child, and their past affairs. 

Mike Kanellis's marital life with wife Maria Kanellis. They are expecting a baby

Mike started dating Maria Kanellis back in June of 2011. They first met at a and they were both in relationships at the time (gasp). 

Mike revealed this information during an interview. He even added that they hit it off right away but were conservative because they were with other people. After just a few months, things changed in both of their lives and they started messaging each other. This eventually lead to Mike traveling nine hours to meet Maria in Philadelphia.  

A year after they started dating, Mike popped the question as they got engaged on 28th November 2012. The couple took their time as they got married almost 2 years later on 10th October 2014 at a ceremony that took place in Chicago.

Maria Kanellis with husband Mike Kanellis at their wedding

 As of 2018, the couple has been married for the past 3 years and are together for almost 7 years. Moreover, they are also expecting their first child. They made the announcement on 25th September 2017 and later on 12th Jan 2018, they also shared that it's a baby girl through WWE's YouTube Channel. 

Just a few months and we will get to see a new member of Kanellis family. Hopefully, they are ready for the struggles of parenthood. 

Update: The pair is parents to a daughter named Fredricka Moon Bennett.

Mike Kanellis and Maria Kanellis past affairs

Talking about Mike's past affairs, there are no reports about him dating someone beforehand. However, we know that he was in a relationship while he met his future wife. It is possible that at the time, he was not ready to share with the world about his former beau or maybe he was not ready for that kind of commitment. 

Now, talking about Maria, she was previously in a relationship with WWE wrestler CM Punk. They started dating back in 2005 and made numerous appearances together on WWE. However, the relationship was not meant to last as they broke up 2 years later in June of 2007.
