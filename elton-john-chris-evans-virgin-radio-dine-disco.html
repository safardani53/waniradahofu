Sir Elton John teams up with Virgin Radio's Chris Evans for Dine &amp; Disco charity event

 Posted on July 1, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  207 words  |  Aldo Pusey

SIR Elton John and Virgin Breakfast’s Chris Evans are joining forces to bring back the station host’s Dine & Disco event.

After a year off it will be held at the Chewton Glen Hotel And Spa, Hants, on Saturday, June 26, with Rick Astley performing.

And the best thing is you can attend for as little as £10 by texting ELTON to 85525, with the money going to the Elton John Aids Foundation.

The names of those donating will go into a prize draw for tickets.

A further 45 tickets will go for auction on The Chris Evans Breakfast show on Thursday.

Attendees will be able to sample food by celeb cooks including James Martin and Tom Kerridge.
