Naomi Campbell boyfriend list: From Skepta to Liam Payne - who has she dated?

 Posted on April 22, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  649 words  |  Aldo Pusey

WORLD-FAMOUS supermodel Naomi Campbell has announced that she is now a mother at the age of 50.

The icon has had some high-profile relationships in the past - from flings with singers and rappers to business tycoons. Here's a look at her past flames...

Who has Naomi Campbell dated?

Liam Payne

Naomi first got together with the former One Direction star in December 2018.

The pair, with a 23-year age gap, were spotted hanging out together in Ghana.

A source told the Sun at the time that Naomi wanted to keep things casual.

They said: "She has said she’s found him a bit too clingy and full-on and does not want a committed relationship at this stage."

The pair called it quits after four months of dating.

Skepta

On January 21, 2018, The Sun revealed how Skepta was secretly dating supermodel Naomi.

She was 13 years older than him.

The fashion star grew close to the grime artist after being introduced by mutual friends.

But the pair are thought to have split in April 2018.

Louis C Camilleri

In 2017, The Sun revealed that Naomi was dating Louis C Camilleri, an Egyptian tobacco company boss 15 years her senior.

At the time he was a director of Ferrari and chairman of Philip Morris International, and worth more than £150million.

Naomi enjoyed secret dates with the businessman and trips on a private jet.

Hassan Jameel

Naomi is thought to have dated Saudi businessman Hassan Jameel in 2016.

Jameel is the Toyota dealership heir, but is best known now as being Rihanna's ex-boyfriend of three years.

Naomi never confirmed her relationship with Jameel, but the pair attended the British Summer Time Festival together in London in 2016.

Vladislav Doronin

Naomi dated Russian businessman Vladislav Doronin from 2008 to 2013.

Vladislav reportedly bought her a Zac Posen dress worth $35,000 at a charity auction, as well as time in the $13,000-a-night presidential suite at the Ritz Carlton Moscow.

But in September 2020, it was reported that Naomi's billionaire ex was sueing her for outstanding loans and property worth $3 million.

When Rihanna started dating Jameel in 2017, she unfollowed Naomi on Instagram, sparking rumours of a feud.

Sean "Diddy" Combs

Naomi and P. Diddy dated briefly in 2002, after Diddy broke up with Jennifer Lopez.

The pair have been friends for years and have made red carpet appearances together, as late as 2017.

Diddy even wore a T-shirt with Naomi's face on it during a holiday to St. Barts.

Flavio Briatore

Flavio Briatore was an Italian businessman and director of Benetton franchises.

Briatore popped the question in March 1999.

The pair never married, and after an on-and-off relationship for four years, they finally separated in 2003.

At the time, Naomi said she refused to be a ‘trophy wife’, claiming in an interview with Marie Claire that her ex had unreasonable expectations.

But Naomi later referred to him as a ‘mentor’ and even joined him and his now ex-wife Elisabetta on holiday in Kenya in 2013.

Adam Clayton

In 1993, U2 bassist Adam Clayton popped the question - just months after he started dating Naomi.

Their first date came after Adam was asked if there was anything in the world he desired but did not have, in an interview, and responded: "A date with Naomi Campbell."

In 2015, Naomi said: "I only have very positive things to say about him. I am very proud of Adam."

Robert De Niro

Naomi is rumoured to have dated legendary actor Robert De Niro in the nineties.
