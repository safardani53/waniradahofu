THE holiday season has arrived - and it's a popular one with the Kardashians.

The mega-famous family, which includes Kris, 68, Kourtney, 44, Kim, 43, Khloe, 39, Rob, 36, Kendall, 28, and Kylie, 26, take Christmas very seriously.

Each year, they show off their over-the-top decor on their social media accounts - especially Kim.

The Skims founder gives her 364 million Instagram followers an inside look at her wildly expensive decorations that are scattered both in and outside her $60 million home in Los Angeles, California.

Take a look at some of Kim's most over-the-top Christmas decor throughout the years.

LOOKING A LOT LIKE CHRISTMAS

This year, the make-up mogul outdid herself by putting 15 massive Christmas trees in her hallway to make it look like a holiday-themed tree farm.

Earlier this week, the Hulu star recorded a video for her Instagram Stories by walking through the grand hallway showing the trees covered in snow.

A pianist could be heard playing holiday music in the background – a tradition the mom of four has been carrying out for years.

The trees reached almost to the ceiling in her home.

Kim's walking tour ended in her living room where the cream-colored piano could be seen.

Next to the piano was a white Christmas tree lit up with yellow-white lights.

CHRISTMAS QUEEN IN THE MAKING

Kim's eldest daughter, North West, 10, also has a winter wonderland of her own inside her huge bedroom.

The beauty expert also shares Saint, eight, Chicago, five, and Pslam, four, with her ex-husband, Kanye West, 47.

In a video shared over the weekend to the mother-daughter duo's shared TikTok account, the pre-teen was decked out head-to-toe in Christmas gear dancing and lipsyncing to a sound that said: "Thanksgiving is over and Christmas is on its way."

In another video, she showed off her holiday-themed room that featured a pink-themed tree that was decorated with all pink embellishments, including pink ornaments, pink bulbs, and pink ribbon.

There were several Christmas presents, wrapped in all-white gift paper, placed underneath the tree.

In addition to the pink tree was a smaller white, silver, and gold-decorated tree.

Another two large Christmas trees were raised on both sides of North's bed and decorated with traditional holiday colors.

On the bed, Santa and elf-shaped pillows were thrown near the head while a plaid blanket was laid on top.

Earlier this week, Kim dropped over $2,500 on gingerbread houses that were designed for each child - and even one for Kanye.

GOING ALL OUT

Last year, North took fans around her home to show off their decorations including gingerbread houses, cookies, peppermint Santa Claus jars, and chocolates hidden in advent calendars.

Other parts of the home remained minimal, including a long, empty, white hallway and another room bare aside from the fireplace and a festive Christmas tree.

Christmas fairy lights and smaller trees were scattered throughout the entire mansion - with personalized trees for each child.

Later on, Kim shared a glimpse of what the outside of her huge house looked like.

The nighttime shot captured every tree along the path perfectly decorated with white lights.

KIM'S KRISTMAS

In 2021, Kim's hair stylist, Chris Appleton flaunted his famous friend's exterior that showed tons of trees with white lights on them.

As he panned around to show how many trees were covered, the hairstylist admitted he was “obsessed with her Christmas tree lights.”

He added: “It gets me so in the mood for Christmas.”

THEY LOOK LIKE WHAT?

Back in 2020, Kim showed off her decorations that featured large white beams, stacked spheres, and trees.

The items were seen lining one of the hallways in Kim's house, where she snapped a photo as she stood in the middle while wearing a matching white dress.

While the decor was made to look as if it was covered in snow, some fans believed it resembled something else - extra large bathroom products.

Earlier this week, the pictures resurfaced on a popular Kardashians internet forum and many were making fun of what her decorations looked like.

One critic said: "NOTHING screams 'The Christmas Spirit' like Kim's giant Tampons and Toilet Paper."

"Their house looks like a hospital," a second said.

Kim's decorations were inspired by Whoville, the fictional town in Dr. Seuss' How The Grinch Stole Christmas.

She also had the Grinch-themed designs in 2019.

A MONOTONE HOLIDAY

In 2020, when it came to Kim's living room, she kept it simple - but also kept the creative juices flowing.

She had stockings for each of the family members hanging with a wood clip on the family’s white fireplace.

On top of the fireplace is the Elf On The Shelf for their two sons and two daughters - except each figurine is inside of a mason jar for proper “quarantine.”

The family’s Christmas tree had artificial snow drooping throughout as warm lights made the branches sparkle. 

ALL OF THE LIGHTS

In 2018, Kim spent her Christmas with Kanye and her three children at the time.

In several photos shared on social media, Kim stunned in a white dress while her rapper ex and kids wore all-black ensembles.
