Tina Turner, the singer, nicknamed the ‘Queen Of Rock & Roll,’ passed away on May 24, 2023, and November 26 would’ve been her 84th birthday. Check out photos of her throughout her career here.

Tina Turner had one of the most iconic voices in music and she sadly passed away on May 24, 2023, at the age of 83. She had her breakthrough with her former husband Ike Turner’s Kings of Rhythm group, which she joined in 1958, before gaining prominence as a solo singer in the 1970s. Her solo debut album ‘Tina Turns the Country On!’ was released in 1974.

She went on to release many other solo albums throughout the years with major hit songs, including her most famous ‘What’s Love Got to Do With It’ off of her fifth album ‘Private Dancer’ in 1984.

Her major impact in the music industry led her to winning multiple prestigious awards, including 12 Grammy Awards, which includes a Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award, as well as an impressive ranking of the 17th greatest singer of all time on ‘Rolling Stone’s 100 greatest singers list. For more photos, check out our gallery here.
