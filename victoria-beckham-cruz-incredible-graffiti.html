Proud mum Victoria Beckham shows off son Cruz's 'incredible' graffiti as he poses with a spray can

 Posted on June 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  421 words  |  Martina Birk

VICTORIA Beckham proudly showed off son Cruz's "incredible" graffiti as he posed with a spray can.

The 46-year-old star was impressed as her youngest son joined acclaimed artist Mijares to create a spray-paint mural in Miami.

She shared a photo of 15-year-old Cruz posing in front of the brightly decorated wall, holding a spray can towards the camera.

Victoria wrote: "Love this", before posting a video of Cruz in action.

The fashion designer added: "This is incredible" as she gushed over her son's artistic skills - with Mijares reposting the clip to his own social media page and adding: "Kid's a natural".

Victoria's final post was a before and after snap of the black wall, which was originally completely plain and surrounded by trees.

In the second image, the trees have been removed and the lower half of the black space is covered in a bright mural.

The mother-of-four praised: "So inspiring!!!"

Earlier this month, Victoria's husband David documented his outing to The Sacred Space Miami, where he and his kids got an art lesson from Mijares.

The former footballer wrote at the time: "Arts and crafts day, special gift from Mijares".

Cruz showed artistic promise at the time, with Becks posting a picture of the teen crouched over his canvas - painting a very modern pop art of a Kaws meets Basquiat inspired teddy bear.

Meanwhile, 18-year-old Romeo painted a footballer, and nine-year-old Harper some books.

David and Victoria's oldest child, 21-year-old Brooklyn, did not attend the activity.

The Beckham family jetted to America last month via private plane, dodging the UK's strict lockdown regulations as they travelled after testing negative for coronavirus.
