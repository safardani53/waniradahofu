Her Career On Television Shows: Xtreme 4x4, Overhaulin, Mythbusters

Jessi started her career with the Speciality Equipment Marketing Association, where she was assigned to build a car in six months. Later, she was cast by the Spike Television on their show, Xtreme 4x4 show alongside Ian Johnson. Working from 2005 to 2008, she presented more than 90 episodes of this show.

In 2009, she appeared in twelve episodes of the seventh season of the Discovery Channel show, MythBusters while its cast, Kari Byron was on maternity leave. She then produced The List 1001 Car Things To Do Before You Die, an award-winning automotive based adventure series in 2011.

As a professional driver, she has participated in various events such as Ultra 4 Stampede, Ultra 4 National Championship, SCORE Baja 1000 and Ultra 4 King of the Hammers, where she also enjoyed many successes.
