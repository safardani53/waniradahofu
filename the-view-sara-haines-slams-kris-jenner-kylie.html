SARA Haines has criticized Kris Jenner's parenting and shared how she sympathizes with the star's daughter, Kylie, during a live broadcast.

The comment was made on Friday's episode of The View while the hosts were discussing Kylie's recent admission to undergoing a boob job.

Sara's co-host, Joy Behar, first brought up the topic, which occurred on the Season 3 finale of the famous family's Hulu series, The Kardashians.

After Joy, 80, explained that Kylie, 25, expressed her regrets on the show about enhancing her breasts as a teenager, the panelists shared their thoughts on the matter.

Sara, 45, was the first time jump in, immediately slamming Kar-Jenner matriarch Kris, 67, for seemingly encouraging her daughters to get plastic surgery.

"I do not know Kris Jenner, but one thing I always took issue with is how these young girls were coming up, and it was almost encouraged as a right of passage in that family," the talk show host ranted.

Her co-hosts, Alyssa Farah Griffin, 34, and Ana Navarro, 51, then interjected with their viewpoints, but Sara again blamed Kris for seemingly making her youngest child feel the need to change her appearance.

"The influence of a mom. Kris Jenner's a momager. I feel bad for their insides because you have someone that tells you, 'you were the way you were supposed to be' -" Sara continued before Joy cut her off.

"Oh, they're fixing their insides too, darling," Joy joked, prompting laughter from the audience.

Kris is also a mother to four other daughters: Kourtney, 44, Kim, 42, Khloe, 39, and Kendall, 27, who have been scrutinized for their major physical transformations since their earlier days on reality TV.

Thursday's episode of The Kardashians was the first time Kylie was open about getting a breast augmentation after years of denying going under the knife.

She revealed that her thoughts on getting the procedure changed after welcoming her now five-year-old daughter, Stormi.

"I would be heartbroken if she wanted to get her body done at 19," Kylie told her BFF, Stassie Karanikolaou, 26.

"She's the most beautiful thing ever."

"I wouldn't touch anything."

Stassie added: "If only we could reverse time."

Kylie chimed in: "I know."
