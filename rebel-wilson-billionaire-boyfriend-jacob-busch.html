PITCH Perfect star Rebel Wilson was dating billionaire Jacob Busch until they went their separate ways.

Rebel, 40, and Jacob, 29, started dating in 2019 after they were introduced by a friend.

Who is Rebel Wilson's ex-boyfriend Jacob Busch?

Jacob Busch is an American businessman and an heir to the Anheuser-Busch fortune.

He was born on May 15, 1991.

The Anheuser-Busch family produces Budweiser, one of America's most popular beers.

He is the next generation to follow in his family's historic footsteps.

Busch attended St Louis University and majored in International Business and Entrepreneurship.

Jacob currently lives in Los Angeles, California.

What does he do for a living?

Jacob launched his own brewery in 2016, called Son's Beer, with his friends Elliott Taylor and Carlo Mondavi.

Jacob told Forbes in 2017 that "creating beer is something that takes a lot of ambition."

"I have all my eggs in this basket and really believe that we will do well, just because of our ambitions, our authenticity and our goal of making friends," he said.

He continued: "Like my Grandfather, August Busch Jr. always said, 'making friends is our business.' That’s what I want to bring back into the community in Napa and eventually throughout the US."

The heir is also involved in multiple business ventures outside of beer.

He told Scenester TV in 2013 that he was once a "big smoker" while promoting e-cigarettes brand CIGIREX.

What's Jacob Busch's net worth?

According to Forbes, Jacob is worth to a whopping $100million.

Busch's family were listed as the 16th richest in America, with a fortune of $13.4 billion.

Jacob's father Peter W. Busch is a descendant of Adolphus Busch who co-founded the Anheuser-Busch brewery in 1852.

How did the pair meet each other?

According to Radar Online, the couple met in 2019 after they were introduced at a Hollywood bungalow by a mutual friend. 

Insider told Radar that" Their first date was at Catch restaurant in Santa Monica. She makes him laugh and he cannot get enough of her!"

What happened at the 2020 Monte Carlo Gala for Planetary Health?

The couple made their red carpet debut at the 2020 Monte Carlo Gala for Planetary Health, hosted by the Prince Albert II of Monaco Global Foundation.

Ahead of the event, the couple looked smitten together in the sunshine, with Rebel covering her eyes in huge shades.

Later, the comedian showed off her slimmer figure after she shed 40 pounds in a gorgeous gown.

Jacob joined her in front of photographers and held his girlfriend by the waist in a classic black and white tux. 

Who did Jacob Busch date before Rebel Wilson?

The heir to the $15billion Anheuser-Busch fortune was previously in an on-and-off again relationship with Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills’ (RHOB) Adrienne Maloof.

The RHOB star and Jacob began the high-profile relationship together in 2013 despite being 28 years apart in age.

"When you meet someone they don't have a number on their forehead," Maloof defended the pair's age gap when she spoke with E!.
