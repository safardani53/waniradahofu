Kylie Jenner Kerfuffle: GRANDPA BRAWLS With Paparazzi After Selfie

 Posted on September 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  315 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Remember those three little girls who tried to get a selfie with Kylie Jenner last week only to be reprimanded with a "Don’t touch me!" from their idol?

Well, they came back determined to get that selfie, and this time, they brought a bodyguard: their grandpa.

It looks like it was a good choice, because the overall-wearing, flashlight-wielding granddad had no qualms getting into an ALL-OUT BRAWL with paparazzi who were getting a little too close to his grandkids.

The amped-up gramp yelled, kicked and screamed at photographers – all the while with a cigarette butt dangling from his lips.

Even after security broke up the brouhaha, the old man put up his dukes and went back for more!

The geriatric Hulk Hogan eventually walked away with the fam, but not before yelling "Suck my c**k!" to the crowd.

Kylie peaced out before the fight came to a head, which is a bummer because it would’ve made a tremendous Snapchat story.

The good news? The girls got their selfie this time. 

The incident took place outside The Nice Guy club in West Hollywood, where the fans and photographers awaited Kylie’s exit.

While waiting, TMZ interviewed the three young fans and asked about the other night when they failed to get a pic with the reality star.

"No, and it really made us upset," one of the girls said.

"Please take a picture with us!" the young fans added, speaking to camera in a message to Kylie. "We love you!"

This confuses us a tad, because in an attempt to defend herself, Kylie tweeted that she had taken selfies with the girls inside the restaurant two nights ago, just after she snapped at them outside.

Maybe she confused them with other fans?

No matter, because the determination of these tweens led to one of the best senior citizen shows we’ve seen in a long time.
