PHARRELL Williams has produced several mainstream hits for a number of artists.

The Happy singer is married to his longtime wife Helen Lasichanh.

Who is Pharrell Williams' wife Helen Lasichanh?

Similar to her hitmaker husband Pharrell, Helen Lasichanh is also a fashion designer and model.

On October 12, 2013, Pharrell and Helen tied the knot in a Miami wedding ceremony.

Regarding their union, The Voice coach told PEOPLE: "I love her because of everything that she is, good, bad, and indifferent.

"You know when I was young I used to say things like, ‘Oh, she’s mine.'

"We share a space in love that is, I don’t possess her. She’s not mine but she sure makes me feel like it."

How did Pharrell meet Helen?

Sometime in the early 2000s, Pharrell and Helen met in Miami via mutual friends.

However, things didn't turn romantic between the two until years later because Helen already had a boyfriend.

The Blurred Lines singer recalled to Oprah Winfrey: "I was just so enamored by the moment I was having with her.

"I was like, 'Who and what is that?' I just knew. I knew at some point something was going to happen.

"She just reminded me so much of myself - different, marches to the beat of her own drum.

"It was different than the rest of the girls who were, like, completely made up. She stood out."

He also remembered things they had in common and shared: "We love music. We're both huge Tribe Called Quest fans."

How many kids do they have?

Pharrell and Helen have a total of four children together.

On November 15, 2008, the couple's first son Rocket was born.

In September 2016, the two revealed they were expecting again.

However, it wasn't revealed how many children they were exactly expecting at the time.

On January 31, 2017, it was announced the pair welcomed triplets together earlier in the month.
