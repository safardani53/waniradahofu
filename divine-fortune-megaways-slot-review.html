What are Divine Fortune Megaways’ bonus features?

Divine Fortune Megaways has a fair amount of bonus resources. While playing the game, you’ll come across three main features: Falling Wilds Respins, Wild on Wild Feature, and Free Spins.

Falling Wilds Respins can be activated by landing a wild symbol on the reels. After wins are paid, the wild symbol moves down one position and triggers a respin. The feature continues until wilds fall from the bottom row.

Players can trigger the Wild on Wild feature by landing a wild on a reel containing a Falling Wild. Doing so makes the wild expand to cover the reel. Wilds keep falling one position, triggering a respin every time they move.

The free spins bonus round can be triggered with four Zeus Lightning Scatters, rewarding players with 10 free spins. Each additional scatter awards five free spins, up to a maximum of 20. 

Wilds automatically expand during the round bonus, triggering the Wild on Wild feature. In addition, the Free Spins feature three coins that can be collected on a special meter. Collecting five coins rewards you with the following prizes:

  • Bronze Coin – 10x your stake
  • Silver Coin – 25x your stake
  • Gold Coin – 500x your stake
