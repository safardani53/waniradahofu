Four New K-pop Female Groups Are Now Conquering The Music Industry

 Posted on April 16, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  652 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

K-pop girl groups are now ruling not just the business but the globe, and fans are loving it. The emerging female groups are putting all they have in order to reach the peak of the music business. To acquire a larger following, these young K-pop female groups, often known as ‘rookies’ in the K-pop market, have experimented with their dancing techniques, fashion sense, and ideas.

Aside from attractive graphics, these rising ensembles have trained and worked really hard to carve out a place for themselves in a highly competitive sector. These four groups’ music, dancing, and general charms have also received accolades and renown.

Let’s take a look at some of the most successful new K-pop female groups in the world.

Fans have been left dumbfounded by these four new K-pop lady groups.

1) NMIXX

NMIXX began on February 22, 2022, as a sub-label of JYP Entertainment called SQY4D. The seven-member group was the first to make its debut since Itzy, a popular female band from the same label, in 2019. Lily, Jinni, Jiwoo, Haewon, Sullyoon, Bae, and Kyujin are all really skilled and have attractive aesthetics. Ad Mare, their first single album, quickly conquered the K-pop industry. K-pop fans were ecstatic to hear the popular song, which established a new record for the trio.

NMIXX sold 227,399 copies on the Hanteo Chart, earning the music chart and data gathering site’s first-ever silver certification. The O.O video, which was uploaded on the ‘STUDIO CHOOM’ channel on February 25, has 73 million views on YouTube.

2) Kep1er

Kep1er, a South Korean female K-pop band, made its formal debut on January 3, 2022, under the management of WAKEONE Entertainment and Swing Entertainment. Mashiro, Huening Bahiyyih, Youngeun, Xiaoting, Chaehyun, Yujin, Dayeon, Hikaru, and Yeseo make up the band’s nine members.

Girls Planet 999, a Mnet survival program, established the blooming female group, which was unveiled after the show’s last broadcast on October 22, 2021. The group’s mini-album FIRST IMPACT and its title single WA DA DA were and continue to be major successes to this day. The amazing ladies also received Brand Customer Loyalty Awards for Rookie of the Year (Female) in 2022, a noteworthy accomplishment for young K-pop girl groups. Doublast, the group’s second EP, is set to be released on June 20, 2022. After becoming models for S2ND, a South Korean cosmetics company, their fame and career skyrocketed.

3) Sserafim’s

Source Music and Hybe Corporation collaborated to create Le Sserafim, which made its premiere on May 2, 2022. The band made news immediately after the release of their album Fearless, which sold over 175,000 copies.

Sakura, Kim Ga-ram, Huh Yun-jin, Kazuha, Kim Chae-won, and Hong Eun-Chae are among the group’s brilliant and gorgeous idols. Le Sserafim added to their celebrity by winning their first song competition on SBS MTV’s The Show. Many people are surprised that the K-pop girl trio accomplished so much so soon after their formal debut.

4) IVE

Starship Entertainment developed the South Korean girl band IVE with some of the most talented performers, including Yujin, Gaeul, Wonyoung, Liz, Rei, and Leeseo. On December 1, 2021, the six-member female band made their debut with the single album Eleven. They also played their chart-topping hit Eleven on KBS2’s Music Bank.

The song peaked at #9 on the World Digital Song Sales chart and spent fourteen weeks on the Billboard Global 200. This was the catalyst for the group’s breakthrough, which included reaching the top of Billboard’s Hot Trending Songs list. The group also charted on the Billboard Artist 100, a notable success for the first K-pop female group.

Eleven is also ranked #4 on Spotify’s Global Viral Top 50, and its official music video has had over 100 million views as of March 8, 2022. IVE’s second single album, Love Dive, received 200 million views for its TikTok challenge only one week after its release, demonstrating the group’s popularity among fans.
