Cheez-It has confirmed that customers can no longer buy a popular flavor at the grocery store.

The snack brand, owned by Kellogg's, is one that's seen in many households today.

But unfroutatnley, new varieties come and go.

One fan recently asked about the availability sweet & salty snack mix on X, formerly known as Twitter.

"I loved those and now I can never find them anymore," the fan added in addition to the question.

In response, Cheez-It took no pride in breaking the news.

"Hey, we are as devastated as you about the Cheez-It Sweet and Salty snack mix being discontinued," the snack brand said.

But the fan wasn't the only one who adored the popular variety of the Cheez-Its.

In a Walmart review of the item, one customer called it the "best boxed snack on the shelf."

Overall, Walmart customers rated it 4.2 stars out of five online.

The snack mix featured popular M&M's milk chocolate pieces, salted pretzels, sugar corn squares, and caramel-flavored popcorn along with the Cheez-it original pieces.

Some other ingredients in the box include artificial and natural flavors, seed oils, and artificial food colorings.

Per serving, there are five grams of added sugar.

In an eight-ounce box, the treat sold for $7.53 at Walmart.

OTHER ALTERNATIVES TO BUY

While the M&M mix is gone, Cheez-It fans might fall in love with other flavor varieties it has on the market.

For example, Cheez-It sells a baked snack mix that includes pretzels, mini toasted bread slices, and cheese-flavored rice puffs.

A 10.5-ounce box sells for $3.68 at Walmart.

There is also a double cheese snack variety that features the same pieces plus cheese curls (instead of rice puffs) and white cheddar squares.

It sells in a 9.75-ounce box at Walmart for $3.68.

Also, M&M's makes its own snack mix with its traditional flavor, peanut butter chocolate, and peanut chocolate candy.

It sells in an 8.3-ounce bag for $4.78.

MORE CHEESE-IT DISCONTINUED ITEMS

But fans might be in for a little more devastation, as Cheez-It has announced the discontinuation of other items recently as well.

This includes cheese pizza and cheeseburger flavors.

Another includes the Puff'd Scorchin' Hot Cheddar snack.

To give it the hot flavor, Cheez-It used paprika extract and natural flavors.

They were a part of the Puff'd lineup that was announced on January 31, 2022.
