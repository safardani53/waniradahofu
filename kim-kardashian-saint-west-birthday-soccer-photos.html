KIM Kardashian has gone all out for Saint West's eighth birthday party, a soccer-themed extravaganza that spared no expense.

Kim, 42, hosted the backyard celebration in her $60 million Hidden Hills, LA mansion, showcasing the opulent affair on her Instagram Stories.

The star of the show, Saint, sporting bleach-blond hair, took a unique approach to his birthday treat – a giant, all-chocolate soccer ball.

Kim's first snap revealed the impressive spherical dessert featuring Saint's name in large letters and delicate white edible flowers.

The twist was Saint dislikes cake, prompting the creation of this unconventional treat.

Captioning the first photo, Kim spilled the beans: "Fun fact - Saint hates cake, so @butterloveandhardwork made an all-chocolate soccer ball instead of a cake! The kids loved it."

KICKING IT WITH SAINT

The celebration continued with Saint donning a soccer jersey and a stylish bleached high-top hairstyle, ready to take on the chocolate creation.

Armed with a mallet, Kim filmed him enthusiastically smashing the exterior of it at her kitchen table.

He unveiled more petite edible soccer balls inside as his pals cheered and grabbed pieces.

Kim's next Instagram Stories showcased her famous attention to detail, from soccer ball water bottles to soccer-patterned holders for bites like onion rings, all expertly crafted by a private chef.

"Everything is soccer-themed!" Kim announced while entering the frame for a brief moment, sporting a cute black jersey herself.

Kim then led her followers to the expansive backyard, where massive inflatables entertained the guests. 

"Look how cute these kicking games are," Kim declared.

The party also included a soccer scrimmage, with a part of her backyard turned into a full-sized pitch.

Psalm West, four, could be seen on the sidelines looking a little nervous about whether or not to join in.

"Does anyone recognize this boy?" Kim asked as she filmed an adorable Psalm with a fresh haircut.

In addition, custom tee shirts for each guest awaited on a rack inside.

They read: "Best In The West" and were made by Addidas.

The lavish bash was a testament to Kim Kardashian's dedication to memorable (expensive) celebrations, even post-divorce from Kanye West in 2022.
