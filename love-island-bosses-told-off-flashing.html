I was on Love Island and bosses told me off for flashing

 Posted on July 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  239 words  |  Martina Birk

LOVE Island 2021 star Abigail Rawlings has admitted she was given a telling off by bosses for flashing her boobs on the show.

Stars are told by producers to "never be fully nude" while filming, but 28-year-old Abigail accidentally disobeyed them.

She told the Daily Star.: "I remember I was sat doing my makeup once and I had my towel and it just fell down a little bit.

"It's all girls so it doesn't really matter but over the speaker they were like 'Abi, can you put your towel on properly please?' And I was like 'Oops, sorry'."

"I think ITV would just say 'don't' because it's annoying for them if they miss anything as we would just chitchat all the time."

Abi was on the summer show in 2021 when she struck up a brief romance with Toby Aromolaran in Casa Amor.

"I’m going to start working with charity organisations. I love animals, I’d love to do anything with dogs."
