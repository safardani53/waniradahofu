Jenni JWoww Farley Hollywood Life

 Posted on February 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  327 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Bio:
Jenni Farley (born Jennifer Farley in Franklin Square, New York on February 27, 1986) better known as JWoww, is a reality TV star who became famous from starring in the MTV reality series, Jersey Shore. In December 2009, 23-year-old Jenni began appearing on the series, which also featured seven other housemates, who spent the summer in Seaside Heights, New Jersey. They made six seasons overall.

Farley launched a line, “Filthy Couture,” in a Las Vegas, Nevada runway show in July 2010. In April 2011, MTV announced that it ordered two Jersey Shore spin-off series, one to be called Snooki & JWOWW, which would star Jenni and Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi. In 2010 Jwoww released a book titled The Rules According to JWOWW: Shore-Tested Secrets On Landing A Mint Guy, Staying Fresh To Death, and Kicking the Competition to the Curb.

In 2012, JWoww and her Jersey Shore costar, Snooki, starred in the 12-episode spin off show, Snooki & JWoww, which premiered on MTV on June 21, 2012. The show follows Snooki and Jenni moving in together and progressing in life, as Jenni gets engaged and Snooki has a baby. The second season of Snooki & JWoww premiered on January 8, 2013. JWoww later returned to her Jersey Shore roots for the revival series, Jersey Shore: Family Vacation, which premiered in April of 2018.

Best Known For:
Being a cast member of Jersey Shore.

Personal Life:
Jenni married Roger Mathews. in October 2015. Jenni confirmed her first pregnancy on Christmas Day, Dec. 25, 2013, and later gave birth to a baby girl, Meilani Alexandra, on July 31, 2014. On her wedding day, Oct 18, 2015, Jenni announced that she and Roger are expecting their second child together and went on to welcome their son Greyson on May 5, 2016. Jenni filed for divorce from Roger in Sept. 2018 and began dating her friend’s brother, wrestler Zack Carpinello, in early 2019. Zack proposed in Feb. 2021 and they are currently engaged.
