Who is Randy Gonzalez's wife?

TikTok star Randy was married to Kimberly Gonzales.

The couple tied the knot in 2018 after a lengthy courtship.

Gonzalez has credited his wife for the amazing support she provided as they sought medical treatment for his colon cancer.

What happened to Randy Gonzalez?

The 36-year-old was battling with stage four colon cancer.

His doctors allegedly gave him five years to live if he underwent chemotherapy, but on Wednesday, January 25, 2023, Gonzalez died in a hospice.

He told his 15 million fans about his diagnosis in April 2022 and provided updates throughout his fight.

According to TMZ, Gonzales set up a GoFundMe to help pay for his care after the University of Texas cancer centre denied him treatment due to his insurance policy.
