Brandy Norwood Biography

 Posted on July 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  322 words  |  Martina Birk

In 1996, Brandy had a romantic love affair with Kobe Bryant. Kobe Bryant is a professional basketball player from United States. She was also seen with him at his prom at Lower Merion High School. However, this love life was short lived.

Brandy also dated Wanya Morris for few years. Wanya Morris is an R&B singer. In the early 2000s, she began dating Robert "Big Bert" Smith. However, she didn't revealed dating him until she announced her pregnancy in 2002. She also claimed that she married him secretly but Smith denied this claim. They broke up in 2004.

In 2004, Brandy was spotted with Quentin Richardson. Quentin Richardson is also a former professional basketball player. He played in the National Basketball Association for several years. They got engaged in 2004 but this engagement was called off in 2005.

In 2010, Brandy was seen with rapper Flo Rida but this love life was not that romantic according to her. Later she was found with Ryan Press. Ryan is a music executive. After being in love for several months, they announced their engagement in December 2012. However, this engagement was also called off in April 2014.

Brandy was born on musical family. Her father is a great gospel singer. As she was raised in a musical background, she began singing gospel solo since she was 2. In 1983, she joined Hollywood High Performing Arts Center. American singer Whitney Houston is her idol and she began following her path to reach her aim. When she was 11, she took part in talent shows. As she was an emerging talent, she signed a recording contract with Teaspoon Productions. She also appeared in R&B boy band Immature as their backing vocalist. Her debut album, Brandy was released on 27th of September 1994. Her first successful album, Never Say Never was released on 9th of June 1998. Her net worth is said to be around $12 million.
