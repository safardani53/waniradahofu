Paul Walker’s Girlfriend Cries After His Death — See The Pic

Jasmine, 23, is turning to her close friends and family, as well as Paul’s loved ones, for support during this difficult time. In one new photo, Jasmine is shown sobbing while a friend holds her in a comforting embrace.

In another, we can clearly see the emotion on her face — her eyes are swollen and red from crying and she has no makeup on. Jasmine is barefoot in the pic, wearing a simple baggy sweatshirt and jeans as she emerges from her car.

Jasmine, who dated Paul on and off for seven years, since she was only 16, is clearly taking his loss really hard, and our hearts go out to her.

Jasmine Pilchard-Gosnell Breaks The Tragic News To Paul Walker’s Daughter

Jasmine may have had one of the hardest tasks of all — telling Paul’s 15-year-old daughter, Meadow Walker, about his death.

“I believe she is the one who told Meadow about what happened, and when she found out she was hysterical and crying,” eyewitness Jim Torp told HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “It was just so sad. I tried to do everything I could to help them. Paul’s girlfriend then took Meadow and drove her home. I don’t know if they drove to crash site. I just know they were in so much anguish.”

On Dec. 4, Paul’s daughter was spotted for the first time since the accident, also looking wracked by grief outside his home.

Our thoughts go out to Meadow, Jasmine and all of Paul’s family and friends.
