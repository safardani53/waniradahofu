Career

Menzel made her public debut as a vocalist in 1998 at the ‘Lilith Fair event. During her nationwide travels, she often performs in New York City. Hollywood Records published her debut solo album, ‘Still, I Can’t Be Still,’ in 1998.

Menzel went on a musical tour to promote ‘I Stand’ in 2008, and the first four cities were perfect sellouts. In November 2008, she sang the album’s title tune in Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade.

‘Holiday Wishes,’ her fourth album, was released in 2014. The album reached number ten on the Billboard 200, making it her highest-charting album.

Idina received the ‘Breakthrough Artist Award’ at the 2014 ‘Billboard Women in Music award event.
Menzel released her fifth studio album, ‘Idina,’ on September 23, 2016.

Her song “Let It Go” from the film “Frozen” earned an Academy Award, and a Grammy Award, and was nominated for a Golden Globe Award in 2014.
