Are Joey King and Jacob Elordi from Kissing Booth still together?

 Posted on July 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  519 words  |  Martina Birk

JOEY King and Jacob Elordi, who star as Elle and Noah in the Kissing Booth film franchise, are no longer together.

The two briefly dated after filming the first installment of The Kissing Booth in 2017.

Joey and Jacob recently opened up to media outlets about how dating in the public eye is "brutal."

Who are Joey King and Jacob Elordi?

Joey King and Jacob Elordi are both actors.

Joey first gained notoriety after she played Ramona Quimby in the comedy film Ramona and Beezus.

Australian actor Jacob rose to fame after he portrayed Nate Jacobs in the hit HBO series Euphoria.

Are they together?

Joey and Jacob began dating after they filmed Kissing Booth together in 2017.

In November 2018, however, rumors surfaced that the pair had split.

Fans speculated that the two parted ways after photos vanished from Instagram feeds and Jacob announced a break from social media.

Around the time they were said to have split, Joey cryptically tweeted "thank God I have my dog though."

The pair recently opened up about dating in the public eye.

"In this world, everyone wants to know your business and what you're doing" Joey, 20, told Entertainment Tonight while promoting the film's sequel.

"And I think it's very interesting when you go through things and people kind of feel like they're owed an explanation, but I understand."

"For me, I've publicly gone through some things, privately gone through some things, it's all about finding your balance, finding what makes you comfortable," Joey continued.

"And it can be brutal, but you know happy endings do come to those who are patient."

What is Kissing Booth?

The Kissing Booth follows the story of Elle Evans, a slightly dorky High School student who has a long-term crush on her best friend's older brother, Noah.

Elle signs up to take part in a charity Kissing Booth, where Noah pays for a smooch.

The second movie sees the relationship struggle as Noah goes off to college, leaving Elle behind in high school.

When is the second movie coming?

The Kissing Booth 2 dropped on Netflix on July 24 after overwhelming popular demand for a sequel.

But fans are already wondering whether there will be a third installment of the quirky Netflix rom-com.

While appearing on the Today Show, Joey spoke about the possibility of a third movie: "Well, me and the cast have been talking and we want that so bad and we’re kind of relying on the fans to help us out.

"They loved the first one so much and they’re the reason that we got a sequel.
