In honor of Kris Jenner’s 68th birthday on November 5, we’re looking back at some of her fiercest outfits ever.

Is there anything Kris Jenner can’t do? The reality star isn’t just a momager, but she is also a serious fashionista and she has continued to prove her flair for fashion. While Kris has rocked a ton of different outfits over the years, there’s no denying her love of a suit. The grandma of over 12’s closet is packed with power pieces like smart jackets and well-tailored trousers. And the matriarch is a master of monochrome, always creating a contrast with her black-and-white looks.

From her sheer shirts to her leather outfits, leopard jumpsuits, & so many more, Kris Jenner has proved age is just a number. You can see all of her sexiest looks when you click through the gallery!
