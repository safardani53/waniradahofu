Who is Andrew Spencer?

Andrew Spencer was one of the men battling it out on season 17 of The Bachelorette.

He is now starring in the Bachelor in Paradise 2022, which premiered on September 27, 2022.

Spencer did not win the heart of Thurston, who appeared on Matt James' recent season of The Bachelor, and was eliminated on July 19.

Spencer, who is from Illinois, plays for the Dacia Vikings in the Austrian Football League.
