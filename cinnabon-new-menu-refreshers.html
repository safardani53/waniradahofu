Fast food chain and rival to Krispy Kreme adds two new menu items - but theres a catch

 Posted on June 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  330 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

A FAST food chain restaurant has announced the addition of two new drink flavors.

Cinnabon is bringing on the two drinks to their menu - but there is a catch in how customers can order the items.

Both drinks are lemonade-based and are called "refreshers."

The menu additions are part of Cinnabon's springtime menu.

The company has not released how long customers have with the two new flavors.

Cinnabon said if customers want to try the sweet new drinks, they can place orders through the online store or third-party apps.

Cinnabon confirmed with The U.S. Sun that both refreshers are also available via Cinnabon’s in-store bakery.

The two new refreshers are strawberry and passionfruit.

1. STRAWBERRY

The Strawberry refresher is infused with a strawberry puree.

The flavor blends with the citrusy lemonade and vanilla for the perfectly light warm weather beverage.

A small costs $4.29 and a regular will set customers back $4.49.

2. PASSIONFRUIT

The passionfruit flavor derives from a puree and is similarly blended with vanilla and lemonade.

According to QSR Magazine, passionfruit flavors have been a hit at other major retailers.

The flavor has seen a 208 percent increase in orders across the retail sector.

MENU CHANGES GALORE

Cinnabon is not the only food chain that is going through menu changes in the new season.

Wendy's is returning their $3 breakfast offering and their popular vanilla frosty drink after customers complained about their favorite menu items.

Taco Bell similarly brought back its beefy crunch burrito after customers complained about its absence.

Meanwhile, Starbucks is discontinuing a fan-favorite refresher as customers wave goodbye to the raspberry-flavored syrup offering.

Starbucks is leaning into their coffee drinks, adding olive oil-infused drinks.
