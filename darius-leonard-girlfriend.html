About Darius Leonard

As already cleared above, Darius Shaquille Leonard, famous as Darius Leonard, plays for the NFL team Indianapolis Colts.

Leonard was born on July 27, 1995, to his parents, in Nichols, South Carolina, as one of his mother’s nine children.

His parents divorced while he was still a kid, so his mother raised him as a single mother.

He is a younger brother to the former American professional football player Anthony Waters.

Darius attended Lake View High School in South Carolina and played football for its team.

Before starting his NFL career, he was a college football player for the South Carolina State Bulldogs.

After the NFL draft, Darius signed a four-year contract worth $7.24 million with the Indianapolis Colts on July 23, 2018.

Darius has already appeared in four seasons for the Colts and is one of the team’s most trusted linebackers in the ongoing 2022 season.

The player has recorded over 530 tackles and ten interceptions in four league season matches.
