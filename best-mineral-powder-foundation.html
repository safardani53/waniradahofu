What is mineral foundation powder?

Mineral foundation powder is mainly composed of good-for-you minerals like clay, zinc oxide, iron oxides, mica, and titanium dioxide.

They're antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and great for blemish-prone, oily skin or combination skin.

Mineral foundations also famously won't contain any oils, waxes, preservatives, synthetic fragrances or bulking agents.

Is mineral makeup really better for your skin?

Mineral makeup is preservative-free and non-comedogenic, which means it doesn't clog pores, which usually causes breakouts.

This makes these products are lot kinder to those with sensitive skin or those who suffer from acne.

So, if you suffer with enlarged pores or breakouts, it's really worth trying mineral makeup.

Many mineral makeup also contain zinc oxide, which is a natural, nontoxic sunscreen to prevent signs of photo-ageing and irritation.

Is mineral powder foundation better than liquid foundation?

If you are prone to breakouts or if you have sensitive skin, it's generally better to stick to powder foundation.

That's because mineral powder lays nicely over your pores and absorbs any oil and perspiration more easily.

Again this means it's less likely to clog your pores and lead to breakouts.

Loose powders are brilliant for blending thanks to ultra-fine pigments, while pressed powders are perfect for no-mess on-the-go touch-ups.
