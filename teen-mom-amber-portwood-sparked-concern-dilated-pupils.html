TEEN Mom Amber Portwood has left fans worried after noticing something strange in the star's new selfie.

The TV personality has followers concerned after she appeared to have dilated pupils in her latest snap, which also featured her in a low-cut dress.

On Saturday, Amber was all smiles as she shared a rare selfie to her Snapchat.

The 32-year-old had her long hair styled in loose waves, and both her lips and cheeks were tinted pink.

She wore a red-and-white polka dot dress that showed off her curves with its low-cut square neckline and short length.

For jewelry, Amber styled her selfie look with a nose ring and a pendant necklace.

Some fans noticed that her pupils seemed to be dilated, though, and debated with one another on an online forum about whether or not they looked “normal.”

A few posters argued that “no,” Amber’s eyes did not look like they should.

One Teen Mom fan said this could be due to a Snapchat filter altering her appearance.

Another claimed any kind of medication could possibly have that affect.

A third shared: “So to be fair my pupils do this. Apparently it does it when I talk about things I’m passionate about and it freaks people out.”

A fourth had a similar thought, though used an animal for their example, instead.

“Her pupils look like my cats' pupils when they're about to pounce on a toy,” the forum user wrote.

Aside from the possible difference in the dilation of her eyes, Amber’s latest selfie also featured another major change from one she posted earlier this month. 

'SO SAD'

The MTV star looked downcast in the at-home photo, which she shared shortly after her ex Andrew Glennon posted a snap with their son James.

Amber pouted while posing in her living room in the snap, which she captioned: "Hump day..."

Her fans flooded the comments section with their concern for the Teen Mom OG star.

One wrote: "You almost always look so sad," with a crying face emoji and another added, "I hope you're doing okay Amber."
