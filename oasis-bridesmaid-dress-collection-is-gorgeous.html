Oasis drops bridesmaid dress collection &amp; there's one MAJOR game changer

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  336 words  |  Aldo Pusey

HIGH-street store Oasis has just dropped a blinder with its first ever bridesmaid dress collection.

The collection of seven dresses will be a hit this summer and there's one to suit every body shape.

Possibly the most exciting frock on offer is the multiway maxi dress in powder pink and soft grey which offers loads of different ways of wearing it.

Say goodbye to petty squabbling, and dress-related secret WhatsApp groups.

This could be the game changer to keep friends not falling out this wedding season.

It's not just about dresses - there are shoes to match as well - making it a one-stop shop for all your bridesmaid needs.

It's no secret that bridesmaids ideally would like to part with cash on a dress that they might wear again, and these ones are guaranteed to be rolled out time and time again.

Prices for the dresses start at £80 and they will be available in sizes 6-18.

Anyone heading to London could organise a personal styling session.

Oasis is now offering bridal parties the chance to try on the dresses at its Tottenham Court Road store - and also experience a prosecco afternoon tea in their Saucer & Spritz café at £25 per person. 

This can be pre-booked online if you fancy a day out in the big smoke.
