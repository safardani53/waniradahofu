How can I get tickets to Janelle Monae's Age of Pleasure Tour?

 Posted on May 2, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  513 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

BELOVED singer Janelle Monáe shocked fans in May 2023 when she announced a new tour in honor of her upcoming album, The Age of Pleasure.

Now, they want to know how they can secure their tickets for her upcoming shows.

When is Janelle Monáe going on tour?

Janelle Monáe is a popular singer, rapper, and actress known for her work in the entertainment industry.

Over the years, the Grammy Award nominee has released five albums, including Metropolis: The Chase Suite (2008), The ArchAndroid (2010), The Electric Lady (2013), and Dirty Computer (2018).

The Age of Pleasure (2023) will be her fifth album to date, and in honor of the special occasion, she is planning to perform shows across Canada and the United States to celebrate with fans.

"Her tour promises to light up your body, soul and all of your senses with an unforgettable performance featuring songs off her latest album and classic hits from her discography," concert promoter Live Nation said in a statement, via 9News.

Monáe will kick off her tour in Seattle, Washington, on August 30, 2023.

How much are tickets and where can I get them?

Tickets for Monáe's tour will go on sale to the general public on June 7, 2023, at 10am local time.

Her tour will also offer several presale options, including one for Verizon Up members, one for Spotify users, one for Live Nation members, as well as one for those who sign up in advance.

Tickets can be purchased online through Monáe's website or on Ticketmaster.
