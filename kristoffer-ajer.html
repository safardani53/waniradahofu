Kristoffer Vassbakk Ajer is a Norwegian professional footballer who plays as a center-back for Premier League side Brentford and the Norway national team.

Kristoffer began playing football with Raelingen FK’s youth team. He joined Lillestrom’s youth team in 2010, and he played there till 2014. He once participated in the Tippeligaen with Start. On February 17, 2016, he committed to a four-year deal with Celtic, and he made his team debut in June of the same year, earning a signing bonus of up to £650.000. During the second leg of the UEFA Champions League qualification phase, on July 20, 2016, he made his club debut. On January 20, 2017, he was loaned to Kilmarnock FC, where he remained till the completion of the 2016–17 campaign, participating in 17 games across all competitions.

Since returning to Celtic, he has established himself as a regular in central defense. On October 24, 2019, he made his 100th appearance for Celtic’s first team in all competitions in the Europa League. On July 21, 2021, he signed a five-year contract with English club Brentford for an unknown amount.

He has represented Norway in several age groups on the international stage. He has played for Norway at the U16, U17, U18, U19, U21, and senior levels. On March 23, 2018, he made his international debut for Norway. He has received almost 20 caps for his country since making his debut.
